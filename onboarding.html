Welcome to NeuroFlex360

Let's keep your brain sharp.

This takes about 10 minutes a day and focuses on three areas:

  • 🧠
    Memory
    Remembering things
  • 🎯
    Focus
    Staying on task
  • ⚡
    Speed
    Quick thinking
🧠

Memory Training

Card matching and pattern recall.

  • 🃏
    Card Matching
    Find pairs of matching cards before time runs out.
  • 🔢
    Sequence Recall
    Watch a pattern light up, then repeat it in the same order.
  • 📍
    Spatial Memory
    Remember which squares lit up on the grid, then tap them again.
🎯

Attention Training

Number focus and visual scanning.

  • 🔢
    Number Focus
    Pick the largest, smallest, even, or odd number from a set.
  • 🎯
    Target Detection
    Tap only when you see the target symbol. Ignore everything else.
  • 🚦
    Selective Filtering
    Tap the correct colour and ignore all the distractors.
⬆️

It Adjusts to You

⬆️ Automatic difficulty
  • Do well two sessions in a row and it gets a little harder.
  • Struggle and it eases back down to help you out.
  • You never need to change anything — it happens on its own.
🔥 Daily Streak
  • Play every day to build your streak.
  • Each day you train, your flame grows.
  • Even one short session keeps it going.

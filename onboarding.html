Welcome to NeuroFlex360

This app helps you train your brain every day.

It takes about 10 minutes per session and works through three simple exercises — memory, attention, and reaction speed.

We will show you how it works, step by step.

🧠

Memory Training

🃏

Card Matching

Flip cards to find matching pairs before the timer runs out

🔢

Sequence Recall

Watch a pattern light up, then repeat it in the same order

📍

Spatial Memory

Remember which positions on a grid were highlighted

🎯

Attention Training

🔢

Number Focus

Pick the largest, smallest, even, or odd number from a set

🎯

Target Detection

Tap only when you see the target — ignore everything else

🚦

Selective Filtering

Tap the correct colour or shape, ignore distractors

⬆️

It Adjusts to You

The app watches how you perform and changes the difficulty automatically.

Do well two sessions in a row and it gets harder.
Find it difficult and it eases off.

You always see your current level on each module card.

📊 Currently: Level 1 · Easy

Train every day to build a streak 🔥. The longer your streak, the better your habit.

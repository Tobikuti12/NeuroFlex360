Why NeuroFlex360

Built for people who need it most

Most brain training apps are built for healthy adults. NeuroFlex360 is different — designed from the ground up for elderly users and stroke survivors.

⬆️
Adaptive Difficulty

The app watches your scores and adjusts automatically. Do well and it gets harder. Struggle and it eases off. You always train at the right level.

👁️
Designed for Elderly Users

Large buttons, clear text, simple navigation. A first-time user can start a session without needing anyone's help.

📊
Clinician Dashboard

Clinicians and caregivers get their own view. See every patient's scores, streaks, and difficulty levels at a glance — no need to ask the patient.

🔥
Daily Streak Tracking

A streak builds every day the patient trains. Missing a day resets it. A simple but effective way to build a daily habit.

🎉
Positive Reinforcement

Every session ends with a celebration. Colourful, encouraging, and warm — because elderly patients deserve more than a plain score screen.

🔒
Secure Accounts

Each patient has their own profile with email verification and password recovery. Clinicians see progress data — never login credentials.

Training Modules

Three areas. Nine tasks.

Each module targets a different cognitive domain and includes multiple task types that unlock as the patient progresses.

🧠
Memory

Trains short-term memory and recall through three distinct exercises that increase in complexity with each level.

Card Matching Sequence Recall Spatial Memory
🎯
Attention

Trains focus, vigilance, and selective attention — the ability to concentrate on what matters and ignore what does not.

Number Focus Target Detection Selective Filtering
⚡
Reaction Time

Measures and trains neuromotor speed. The patient taps when a circle turns green. At higher levels the timing becomes less predictable.

5–7 Rounds Variable Delay ms Precision
